Golem Quest

Pick your golem and vanquish the enemies. Two players can work together! Bump into stuff to interact (attack, pick up).
Made in 7 days (hopefully) for the 2014 7DRL Challenge.

Unfortunately you can't play because your browser or GPU driver doesn't support WebGL.

Your browser does not seem to support gamepads, so you can only play with keyboard.

If you have a gamepad, press a button to activate it!
